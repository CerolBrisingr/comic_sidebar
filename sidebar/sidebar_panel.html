Avoid
Most cases of referral links will not work properly. Avoid URLs with a format like:
http://some.page/comic/latest_page.html
Use
Use a direct link. The first page of a online comic or novel will usually do fine. The second-to-last page is a good candidate as well!
http://some.page/comic/page_1.html
http://some.page/comic/page_132.html
